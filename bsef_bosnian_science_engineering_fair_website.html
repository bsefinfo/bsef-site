Bosnian Science & Engineering Fair (BSEF)

The first-ever science and engineering fair in Bosnia and Herzegovina — a celebration of innovation, creativity, and youth potential. Happening on December 14, 2025 in Mostar, Bosnia and Herzegovina, open to all Bosnian schools and students passionate about STEM.

Register Your Project

About the Event

BSEF is the first major scientific and engineering fair ever held in Bosnia and Herzegovina and across the Balkans. It aims to unite young innovators, educators, and researchers to showcase projects that can shape the future.

The event welcomes participants from all over Bosnia — with invitations and registration links sent to every school in the country. Whether you are a student, mentor, or educator, BSEF gives you the opportunity to share ideas, connect, and be part of a growing movement of young scientists.

  • Who can participate: Students aged 13–19, teachers, mentors, and science clubs.
  • When: December 14, 2025.
  • Where: Mostar, Bosnia and Herzegovina.
  • What to expect: Science projects, prototype showcases, workshops, inspiring talks, and awards for the most creative innovations.

Schedule

A one-day event celebrating creativity, research, and collaboration.

  • Morning: Opening Ceremony, Keynote Session, and Project Presentations.
  • Afternoon: Workshops, Innovation Panels, and Award Ceremony.

Register Your Project

Registration is open for all Bosnian schools! Teams can submit projects in categories such as Biology, Physics, Chemistry, Computer Science, and Engineering.
